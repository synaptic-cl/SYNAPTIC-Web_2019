Simplified application system (Sistema de Postulación Simplificada)

Client: Sercotec

We helped SERCOTEC with the creation of a web responsive platform, that would present available competitive funds and allow users to apply with their projects, by completing a form that would later be reviewed and evaluated by Sercotec.
AWS technology used: EC2, S3, cloudwatch, lambda

Online lending (Crédito Digital)

Client: Caja los Andes

Web responsive application that allowed affiliates to request pre-approved consumer loans in an online manner, from simulating the amount, it's monthly payments, and entering user data, to sending the loan request.
Technology used: GCP

Technical Info (Infotécnica)

Client: Coordinador

Web platform used for the administration and visualization of technical information from the installations of companies belonging to the national electrical system. This platform allows access to technical information from centrals, lines, substations and other installations from the companies in the national electrical system.
Technology used: AWS
